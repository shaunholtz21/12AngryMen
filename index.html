🏈 Week 17 — The Angry Bowl V

Team Baune (91.82) vs. 💥 SHOULDA PLAYED THE OTHER GUY (152.18)

Result: Shoulda Played The Other Guy wins by 60.36
Mood: When one team brings a fire hose and the other brings a sparkler.

Team Baune came into the Angry Bowl looking for one more surge, but the lineup never found its footing. Shough (21.92) delivered a strong performance, and Harold Fannin Jr. (11.0) held up his end, but the supporting cast couldn't keep pace. Achane (14.2) and Cook (8.7) landed below expectations, the WR group combined for just 13.7 points, and the Broncos D/ST (7.0) couldn't swing momentum.

Shoulda Played The Other Guy showed up like it was personal. Derrick Henry detonated for 45.6 — the kind of championship swing that ends arguments — while McBride (23.6) and Wan'Dale Robinson (22.3) piled on. It was a complete, top-to-bottom title-winning performance.

Sometimes the Angry Bowl is a nail-biter. Sometimes it's a statement. This one was a statement.

Bench Regrets:
Team Baune: Cam Little (14.0) was the only notable upgrade, but it wouldn't have changed the outcome.
Shoulda Played The Other Guy: Trevor Lawrence (23.12) and Quentin Johnston (14.8) were strong options, but the starters already sealed the deal.
