Draft Recap

🏆 GOAT – The Power Builder
Drafted like they were assembling a tank: deep RBs, strong QBs, and a rookie TE with upside. WRs are a bit of a patch job, but if even one hits, this team could roll over opponents like it’s Week 8 already.
Projection: Playoff lock if WRs cooperate. Might trade a QB just to flex.
❓ Shoulda Played The Other Guy – The Gambler
This roster feels like a coin flip — heads, it’s a playoff team; tails, it’s waiver wire Wednesday by Week 3. Solid RBs and QBs, but WRs and TEs are riding the hope train.
Projection: Fringe playoff team. Needs one breakout WR and a little luck.
🧨 HuPhers – The WR Avalanche
Wide receivers everywhere. If this team scores under 100, it’s because the RBs took a nap. If they score over 150, it’s because the WRs threw a party and invited Lamar.
Projection: High ceiling, high drama. Could lead the league in WR points and post-game victory GIFs.
💥 Hooligans – The RB-Centric Grinder
Built like a fantasy football throwback — run the ball, play defense, and hope the WRs show up. If one of the QBs clicks and the TE room doesn’t implode, this team could grind out wins.
Projection: Competitive, but WR depth needs CPR.
🍻 Dirty Beers – The Rookie Ranch
Drafted like they just watched the NFL Combine on repeat. Mahomes is the adult in the room, surrounded by rookies who might be brilliant or still learning how to line up.
Projection: Sleeper team. Could explode midseason or implode by Halloween.
🔢 Hankson – The Balanced Risk-Taker
This team has a plan — and it mostly makes sense. Strong core, a few calculated risks, and enough depth to survive a bye week apocalypse. If the rookies hit, Hankson’s going deep.
Projection: Playoff potential. Could be the team nobody wants to face in Week 13.
🧱 The Max – Blueprint Perfectionist
Drafted like they had a spreadsheet, a backup spreadsheet, and a vibe check. No glaring holes, no wild reaches — just clean, consistent picks. If this team loses, it’ll be because of a kicker.
Projection: Top-tier contender. Built for the long haul.
🧊 Baune – Upside Over Stability
This team bet on youth, tight ends, and potential. If the breakout WRs deliver, Baune’s in business. If not, they’ll be streaming WRs like it’s a hobby.
Projection: Needs early wins. Could surge late if upside hits.
🔐 The Prison Wallets – Ground Game Specialist
RBs everywhere. WRs? Not so much. If the league shifts toward run-heavy scoring, this team’s golden. If not, they’ll be trading RBs like they’re NFTs.
Projection: Trade candidate for WR help. Could grind out wins with volume.
🧠 Corbett – Air Raid Architect
WRs stacked like a fantasy buffet. QB and TE are elite, and RBs are just there to fill the flex. If WRs stay healthy, this team might not lose a game — or at least not quietly.
Projection: Championship ceiling. Needs RBs to stay functional.
🦅 Parker – The Philly Fan Club
Drafted like they were tailgating in Week 1. WRs are strong, RBs are decent, and the roster has a clear identity. If Philly plays well, Parker wins. If not, it’s going to be a long season.
Projection: Solid playoff team. Needs RB consistency and a few lucky breaks.
🌿 Greenwell – High Variance Wildcard
Elite talent meets deep sleepers. This team could be 5–0 or 0–5 and still feel like it’s one week away from greatness. If the breakout picks hit, Greenwell’s a problem.
Projection: Boom-or-bust. Could lead the league or be the best team in the consolation bracket.
