Mood: One team brought a sledgehammer, the other brought a folding chair

One team dropped bombs. The other dropped the ball.

Bench Regrets:
Hooligans: Kraft (10.6) and Jennings (6.4) were solid, but irrelevant in a blowout.
Team Hankson: Franklin (12.4), Charbonnet (TD), and Mooney (19.84) all outscored starters — but the gap was too wide.

Hooligans didn’t just win — they flexed. When your WR2 drops 33 and your RBs combine for 50+, you’re not playing fantasy — you’re hosting a seminar. Team Hankson had the bones, but no bite. Next week: check your FLEX, and don’t let Mooney rot on the bench again.
