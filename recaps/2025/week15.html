🏆 ROAD TO THE ANGRY BOWL 🏆

Round 15 • Winners Bracket

Six teams enter the postseason, but only four take the field in Round 15. Shoulda Played The Other Guy and Dirty Beers watch from the mountaintop, their byes earned, their confidence high. Below them, four challengers step into the fire — one round, one shot, one chance to keep the dream alive.

The Road to the Angry Bowl begins with survival.

The Trek to Irrelevance begins with one goal: don’t sink any further.
